## How to Use

### Opening the Slides
1. **Local Viewing:** Double-click `EC.html` to open in your default browser
2. **Best Experience:** Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
3. **Mobile:** Works on tablets and smartphones with touch gestures

### Navigation Controls
- **Next/Previous Buttons:** Click buttons at bottom corners
- **Keyboard Shortcuts:**
  - `→` or `↓` or `Space` - Next slide
  - `←` or `↑` - Previous slide
  - `Home` - First slide
  - `End` - Last slide
  - `N` - Toggle speaker notes visibility
- **Touch/Swipe:** Swipe left for next, right for previous (on mobile/tablet)

### Speaker Notes
- Press `N` key to show/hide speaker notes
- Speaker notes contain 200-500+ word narration scripts for each slide
- Perfect for AI video generation tools (Gemini, etc.)
- Includes context, explanations, and delivery guidance

### Exporting to PDF
1. Open `EC.html` in browser
2. Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
3. Select "Save as PDF" as printer
4. Adjust margins if needed
5. Save - creates a PDF with all slides and speaker notes

### Using with AI Video Tools
1. Upload `EC.html` to AI video generation tool (Gemini, etc.)
2. The tool reads both slide content and speaker notes
3. Generates narrated video presentation automatically
4. Speaker notes are comprehensive enough for quality narration
