## How to Use

### Online Viewing (Recommended)
1. Click any branch link above to view slides in your browser
2. Use arrow keys or on-screen buttons to navigate
3. Press **'N'** key to toggle speaker notes

### Local Viewing
1. Clone this repository: `git clone https://github.com/tarakbluru/engineering-branch-slides.git`
2. Navigate to `slides/` folder
3. Open any `.html` file in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

### Navigation Controls

**Keyboard:**
- `→` `↓` `Space` - Next slide
- `←` `↑` - Previous slide  
- `Home` - First slide
- `End` - Last slide
- `N` - Toggle speaker notes

**Mouse/Touch:**
- Click navigation buttons at bottom
- Swipe left/right on touch devices

**Progress:**
- Progress bar at top shows completion
- Slide counter (e.g., "5 / 20") in top-right corner

### Exporting to PDF
1. Open slide deck in browser
2. Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
3. Select "Save as PDF"
4. Choose landscape orientation for best results

## Content Structure

Each branch slide deck includes:

1. **Title & Overview** - Introduction to the branch
2. **Curriculum (Years 1-4)** - Complete course structure and subjects
3. **Skills Development** - Technical skills, tools, and software
4. **Career Paths** - Entry-level to senior roles with salary ranges
5. **Industry Sectors** - Where graduates work and top companies
6. **Higher Education** - MS/PhD pathways and specializations
7. **Day-in-the-Life** - Realistic work scenarios
8. **Compensation** - India and international salary data
9. **Current Trends** - Technology trends shaping the field
10. **AI Impact** - How AI is transforming the branch
11. **Future Strategy** - Skills to develop for long-term success
12. **Educational Resources** - YouTube channels, courses, books
13. **Real Projects** - Student and industry innovations
14. **Honest Assessment** - Pros, cons, and who thrives
15. **Decision Framework** - Self-assessment questions
16. **Summary & Next Steps** - Key takeaways and actions
